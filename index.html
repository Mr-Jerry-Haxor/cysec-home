About Cysec

What is CYSEC?

CYSEC at GITAM University across Vizag, Hyderabad, and Bangalore campuses. We educate about cybersecurity, raise awareness of cyber threats, and empower students to protect our digital future.

Our Vision

At CYSEC, we aim to unite GITAM University students across campuses, educating them about cybersecurity, raising awareness about cyber threats, and fostering expertise to ensure a safer digital world.

Our Mission

At CYSEC, the GITAM University student club, our mission is to empower students with cybersecurity knowledge, raise awareness about cyber threats, and promote secure digital navigation

Frequently Asked Questions

1) Do teachers intervene in this club while teaching CyberSecurity?
No, CYSEC is entirely student-run. We believe in a peer-driven learning model, where students take the lead in sharing knowledge and insights about cybersecurity. While teachers may offer guidance and support, we emphasise students' teaching and learning from each other.

2) Do we require heavy knowledge of coding to excel in CyberSecurity?
Not necessarily. CYSEC welcomes students from various backgrounds and skill levels. While coding can be an asset in certain aspects of cybersecurity, our club is designed to accommodate individuals with diverse skill sets. We offer opportunities to learn coding and other necessary skills as part of our activities.

3) Can students of any school and stream join CYSEC?
Absolutely! CYSEC is open to students from all schools and streams, regardless of their previous exposure to cybersecurity. We believe that diversity enriches our community and encourages a broader perspective on cybersecurity issues.

4) What are the prerequisites to be done before starting with this stream?
There are no strict prerequisites to join CYSEC. All you need is an interest in cybersecurity and a willingness to learn. We provide resources and support to help you get started, regardless of your background.

5) While learning this stream, does my working environment get hacked?
No, your working environment is not at risk during your participation in CYSEC. We prioritize ethical and responsible learning. Our activities are focused on understanding cybersecurity to prevent and mitigate hacking and cyber threats, not engage in harmful activities.

6) What software is meant to be installed before starting?
The specific software requirements may vary depending on your activities within CYSEC. We will provide guidance on recommended software installations, and many cybersecurity tools are freely available. You won't need any specialized software to get started, and we'll support you in setting up the necessary tools as you progress in your cybersecurity journey.
